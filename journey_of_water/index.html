Dreamtime Connections

"The Dreamtime: Local Aboriginal history teaches that the Reef and the Wetlands are one. Ancient stories tell of the Rainbow Serpent carving the rivers that feed the reef, reminding us that if we poison the water here, we poison the spirit of the sea."
